\section*{Product Norm}

\noindent\textbf{Definition:} Let $(E_j, \|\cdot\|_{j})$, $1 \le j \le m$, be normed vector spaces over $\mathbb{K}$. Then
$$ 
\|X\|_{\infty} := \max_{1 \le j \le m} \|x_j\|_j 
$$ 
where $X = (x_1, \ldots, x_m) \in E := E_1 \times \cdots \times E_m$.

This defines a norm, called the product norm, on the product vector space $E$.

The metric on $E$ induced from this norm coincides with the product metric, when $d_j$ is the metric induced on $E_j$ from $\|\cdot\|_j$.
